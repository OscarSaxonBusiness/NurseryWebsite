👋Welcome

Sylvia's Happy Childcare is a residential based EYFS nursery located in Queens Park, London. Our home away from home for children aged 1-4 years old has dedicated and experienced staff who support and encourage the children while they learn, play and explore in our expansive indoor and outdoor settings.

We are open 50 weeks of the year including school holidays. Monday to Friday from 8:00am - 6:00pm

Sylvia's Happy Childcare is owned and operated by Sylvia and Thomas Walkowiak, with a wealth of experience and qualifications between them in Early Childhood Education and Child Psychology.

Our nursery also provides homemade meals which are prepared daily using fresh organic ingredients by our experienced and qualified chef, Thomas Walkowiak. As well as catering for all dietary requirements and needs, our kitchen has also achieved a five-star food hygiene rating from the UK Food Standards.

To arrange an appointment to view our nursery or for more information please don't hesitate to contact us.

Meet Sylvia

Sylvia is the founder and registered manager of Sylvia’s Happy Childcare. With many years of experience working with young children and their families, she is passionate about creating a warm, nurturing and fun environment where every child feels valued and confident.

Her approach is inspired by the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) framework, focusing on each child as an individual learner. Sylvia works closely with parents and carers to build strong, trusting relationships and to ensure that every child’s needs, interests and milestones are carefully supported.

You will often find Sylvia down on the floor playing, reading stories, leading songs or exploring the garden with the children – she believes that the best learning happens through play, curiosity and laughter.

Meet the Team

Our team is made up of caring, qualified and experienced early years practitioners who share the same values: kindness, patience and a genuine love for working with children.

Each member of staff holds relevant early years qualifications and regularly takes part in ongoing training in areas such as safeguarding, first aid, special educational needs, communication and language development, and positive behaviour support.

We keep our ratios low to ensure that children receive plenty of one-to-one attention and emotional support throughout the day. The team works closely together to plan exciting activities, observe children’s progress and celebrate every achievement, big or small.

🏡Our Facilities

Sylvia’s Happy Childcare is based in a welcoming residential setting that has been thoughtfully adapted for young children. Our rooms are bright, cosy and filled with age-appropriate toys, books and resources that encourage creativity, problem solving and imaginative play.

Children benefit from:

  • Dedicated playrooms for different age groups and activities
  • A well-equipped outdoor area for daily fresh air and physical play
  • Quiet, comfortable spaces for rest, naps and story time
  • A safe, secure environment with child-friendly furniture and equipment

We carefully design our spaces so that children can independently explore, make choices and develop confidence while always being supervised by our team.

🥦Nutrition

We believe that healthy, delicious food is an essential part of a happy childcare experience. All meals and snacks are freshly prepared on site by our chef, Thomas, using organic and high-quality ingredients wherever possible.

Our menus are designed to be balanced and nutritious, introducing children to a variety of flavours, textures and cuisines while keeping favourites like hearty soups, wholesome pastas and fresh fruit on regular rotation.

We are able to cater for allergies, intolerances and cultural or religious dietary requirements, and we work in partnership with parents to make sure every child’s individual needs are respected. Meal times are relaxed, social moments where children are encouraged to try new foods and develop independence, such as pouring drinks and serving themselves with support.
